# Greek Salad

A refreshing Mediterranean salad with crisp vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and a simple olive oil dressing.

## Ingredients

### For the Salad
- 4 large tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 English cucumber, sliced
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, cut into rings
- 1 cup Kalamata olives
- 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 2 tbsp fresh oregano leaves

### For the Dressing
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Prepare vegetables**: Cut tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and bell pepper as specified.

2. **Make dressing**: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.

3. **Combine salad**: In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, bell pepper, and olives.

4. **Add dressing**: Pour dressing over vegetables and toss gently to coat.

5. **Add cheese**: Top with crumbled feta cheese and fresh oregano.

6. **Serve**: Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 hours before serving.

## Notes

- Use the best quality olive oil and feta you can find
- Don't overdress - the salad should be lightly coated
- Can be made without cheese for vegan option
- Traditionally served as a side dish but can be a light main course

## Time

- Prep: 15 minutes
- Total: 15 minutes

## Servings

4-6 servings
